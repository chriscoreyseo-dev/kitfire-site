Privacy Policy

KitFire AI Inc. · Flushing, Michigan · Last updated July 20, 2026

This policy covers the kitfire.ai website, our AI phone line, and the KitFire Panel browser extension. The short version: we collect only what you deliberately hand us, we use it only to do the work you asked for, and we do not sell it to anyone.

The KitFire Panel browser extension

What it collects:

  • Text you choose to capture. When you click "Read this page" or drop in a file, that text is sent to KitFire's servers to produce the output you requested (a triage, a summary, a draft). Nothing is captured until you click.
  • Your access code. Sent with each request to verify you are a KitFire customer. It is stored locally on your machine, never on a third-party service.

What happens to it: captured text is processed to generate your output and is not stored on our servers. We retain usage metadata only — which task ran and its size (token counts) — for service metering and billing. Text processing uses Anthropic's Claude API as a subprocessor; Anthropic does not train on API data.

What the extension never does: it does not read pages in the background, track your browsing, collect passwords or payment details, or run any code from outside its own package.

The website

When you submit our intake or signup forms, we store what you typed (name, contact details, and what you told us about your business) so we can respond. Our chat assistant processes what you type to it in order to answer; conversations are not used for advertising. We currently run no third-party analytics or advertising trackers.

The phone line

Calls to our AI-answered line are handled by our telephony provider and may be transcribed and summarized so we can follow up on your request. The assistant discloses that it is an AI.

Payments

Purchases are processed by Stripe. Your card details go to Stripe, not to us — we receive confirmation of payment and the details needed to deliver your service.

What we never do

  • We do not sell or rent your data to anyone.
  • We do not use your data for purposes unrelated to the service you asked for.
  • We do not use your data to determine creditworthiness or for lending.
  • We do not run third-party advertising or hand your information to ad networks.

Retention & removal

We keep customer records for as long as you are a customer plus what tax and accounting law requires. Want something deleted? Email us and we will delete it unless the law says we must keep it.
