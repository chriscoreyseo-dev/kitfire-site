The problem, trade by trade

The phone doesn't stop. Somebody has to answer it.

HVAC & plumbing: the 11pm emergency

A dead furnace in January or a burst pipe on a Saturday doesn't wait for Monday. Whoever answers that call gets the job — and if it rings to voicemail, it rings to your competitor next.

Roofing: the storm-season surge

One hail event and your phone gets a week's worth of calls in a day. Most go unanswered while you're on a roof, and those homeowners don't wait — they call the next name on the search results page.

Electrical: the quote that goes cold

A panel upgrade or an EV charger install is a considered purchase — the homeowner is comparing quotes. Whoever follows up first, and keeps following up, usually wins the job.

Remodeling & custom builders: the long decision

A kitchen or addition is a five-figure decision that takes weeks or months to close. Lose touch for two weeks and the homeowner has moved on to a builder who stayed in front of them.
