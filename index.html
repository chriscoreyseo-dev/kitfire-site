Straight answers

It goes on the board. A supervisor audits the fleet daily, misses get flagged, a human corrects, and the override is logged publicly. Scroll up — the Override log on this page is the real record. The failure-and-fix loop is the product.

Is my data and my business safe?

Nothing irreversible moves without your yes — money, contracts, deletions, publishing. Your accounts, ad accounts, and billing stay in your name. KitFire never holds your credentials.

Do I need to learn prompts?

No. That's the whole bet. KitFire is an operator you hire, not a skill you study. It does the doing; you approve and watch the number.

What does the human still do?

Approves anything that can't be undone, sets the rules, and overrides when quality slips. That line never moves — it's a feature, not a limitation.

Which AI models does it use?

Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, and whatever ships next — the best model on every play. When a better model drops, your roster upgrades without you touching anything.
