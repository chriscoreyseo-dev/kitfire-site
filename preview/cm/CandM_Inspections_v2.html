Why Inspections Matter

Three reasons every athletic director schedules this.

One is the law. One is liability. One is just common sense.

01

It's the Code

ICC 300-2017 requires annual inspection and a permanent compliance plaque listing the inspector and date. Most Michigan school districts include it in their annual safety audit.

02

It's the Liability

A bleacher failure during a game can mean injuries, lawsuits, and personal liability for the AD who skipped the inspection. The annual visit costs less than one hour of legal defense.

03

It's the Money

Catching a worn cable, a stressed weld, or a failing motor early costs hundreds. Replacing the whole system costs tens of thousands. Inspection is the cheapest insurance you'll buy this year.

Every inspection. Every system. No shortcuts.

National Code
ICC 300-2017

Our 12-point checklist meets or exceeds the ICC 300-2017 national standard for bleacher inspection. Every item documented. Every defect photographed. Every report signed.

After inspection, we issue a written report, attach a permanent compliance plaque to the bleacher, and file a copy for your district records.
