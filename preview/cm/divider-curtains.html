Overview

Gym Divider Curtains from C&M.

Divider curtains turn one gymnasium into two, three, or four independent practice courts — multiplying your facility's utility without expanding the building. We install motorized and manual systems, custom-printed school logos available, and service legacy curtains back to the 1990s.

The Problem

When divider curtains call for service

Frayed Curtain Edges

High-traffic gyms wear the bottom edge of the curtain where it drags on the floor. Replacement panels or edge re-binding extends the life by years.

Failed Motor Drives

Motorized curtain drives use the same kind of winch as bleachers — and fail the same way. Replacement is a 4-hour job with the right parts.

Logo Discoloration

Custom-printed school logos fade with UV and washing. Re-printing onto the existing curtain is faster and cheaper than full curtain replacement.

Track Misalignment

Wall-mount curtain tracks can drift over years of cycling. Re-shimming and re-anchoring usually fixes it.

Frequently Asked

Questions ADs ask about Gym Divider Curtains.

How many divider curtains can a gym support?
Most full-size high school gyms support 2–3 divider curtains, creating 3–4 practice courts. Larger field-house facilities can run 4–5 curtains for college-level multi-court operation.
Can divider curtains have our school logo?
Yes. We work with Performance Sports Systems to print custom logos directly on the curtain — vibrant color, machine-washable. Adds about 2 weeks to delivery.
How long does a curtain installation take?
1 day per curtain for wall-mount installation. 2 days per curtain if ceiling-mount track work is required. Most schools schedule installs during break weeks.
Do motorized curtains require electrical work?
Yes — dedicated 120V or 240V circuits depending on the motor. We coordinate with your district's electrician or bring in our own subcontractor when needed.
