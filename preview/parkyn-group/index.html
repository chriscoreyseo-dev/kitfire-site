Meet Kevin

Coaching where leadership actually happens.

Kevin Parkyn has spent more than three decades inside the organizations everyone else studies from a distance — pioneering the first small-teams approach on an American auto plant floor, building safety leadership across a multi-state transportation company, and developing executives at companies like Whirlpool, Henkel, and Deloitte.

As a former Master Trainer and Consultant at DDI (Development Dimensions International) — the global leadership firm working across borders and languages — Kevin doesn't coach from theory. He works alongside managers and executives in real-time, day-to-day situations, so the skills they learn become the habits their teams feel: motivation, accountability, and engagement that hold up under pressure.

His conviction: live a legacy worth passing on to others — in your industry, your community, and your family.
